FAQs

What credit score do I need to apply for a credit card?

A good to excellent credit score (typically 670 or above) increases your chances of approval for premium credit cards.

How can I apply for a credit card online?

Visit our website, fill out the application form, and submit the required documents. It's quick and easy!

Are there any annual fees associated with the credit card?

Fees vary based on the credit card type. Some of our cards have no annual fees, while others offer premium benefits at a nominal cost.

How long does it take to receive the credit card once approved?

Typically, it takes 7-10 business days to receive your card after approval.

How can I check my credit card balance and transactions?

You can check your balance and transactions via our mobile app or online banking portal.

What should I do if my credit card is lost or stolen?

Report the lost or stolen card immediately through our mobile app or customer service hotline for a replacement.

Is my credit card information secure?

Yes, we use state-of-the-art encryption and fraud detection systems to ensure your information is secure.
